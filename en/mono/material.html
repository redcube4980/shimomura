Materials and servicesMaterials and services

We provide the Shimomura brand through the manufacture of high-quality products and development of new products. We also enhance human resources and services and provide products and reassuring support that customers need.

Materials

High-Quality Steel Materials
As a member of the Daido Steel group, Shimomura is able to purchase strictly managed high-quality steel materials from Daido Steel, and with our own technological capabilities cultivated for more than 70 years, we have supplied the highest-quality grinding products that satisfy customer needs.
New Product Development
With support from the Research and Development Division of Daido Steel, we develop materials and propose new steel types according to market needs. We also work with Daido Steel concerning the quality of materials and technological aspects to respond customer opinions and supply products that satisfy customer needs.

Services

Provision of the Shimomura Brand
We always provide reliable services from the supply of materials tailor-made to customer needs to manufacturing technology and quality assurance from the customer’s perspective. In addition, we focus efforts on making rapid responses concerning investigations, improvement, and countermeasures and our internal quality improvement team is taking prompt action.
